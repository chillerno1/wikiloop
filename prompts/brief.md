# Generate a briefing

Based on everything currently in `wiki/`, write a 500-word executive briefing on:

> <TOPIC>

Structure:
1. **Current state** — what the wiki says is true today
2. **Key tensions** — where sources disagree, or where the wiki flags contradictions
3. **Open questions** — what the wiki doesn't yet answer
4. **Recommended next steps** — what to ingest, research, or decide

Cite every claim with `[Source: page-name]`. Use only wiki content — do not pad with general knowledge. If the wiki is thin on this topic, say so and name which focus area needs more sources.

Save the finished briefing to `outputs/brief-<topic-slug>-<YYYY-MM-DD>.md`.
