# Explore unexplored connections

Read `wiki/index.md` and identify the 5 most interesting unexplored connections between existing topics in the knowledge base. For each connection:

1. Name the two (or more) pages it links.
2. Explain what insight the connection might reveal.
3. Suggest one specific source that would help confirm or flesh it out.

Prefer non-obvious links. A connection that's already represented by a `[[wiki-link]]` doesn't count — look for implicit ones the wiki hasn't made explicit yet.
